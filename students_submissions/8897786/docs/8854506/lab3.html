My takeaway on linear regression and univariate linear regression

In essence, simple linear regression and univariate linear regression are interchangeable terms. They both denote a linear regression model involving a single independent variable and a single dependent variable.

In my perspective, univariate linear regression is a valuable and straightforward approach to examine the connection between two variables. It is extensively employed and offers valuable insights into the magnitude and direction of the relationship, enabling predictions based on observed data. Nevertheless, it may have limitations in capturing complex relationships or when multiple predictors are involved, necessitating the use of more advanced techniques.
